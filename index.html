Cable Box Alternative

Your BlackBox is can be a cable replacement. It contains the apps to stream sports, TV episodes or any other content you decide to store on it.

The BlackBox is a PC. If you'd like to browse the internet with it, all you'll need is a mouse and keyboard.

Play From Smartphone, Tablet, Keyboard/Mouse

The BlackBox comes with a small tablet to be used as a remote. It has all the apps you signed up for pre-installed.

Alternatively and conveniently, you may install the apps you need yourself to communicate with your device on your smartphone. A keyboard and mouse are also options.

Choose Your Apps

From NHL Gamecenter to MLB.TV, your BlackBox can support a variety of devices. For a complete listing of currently supported applications, visit the 'Apps' page.

Choose Your Case

BlackBox supports a wide variety of Mini-ATX style cases in a multitude of colors to fit your personal home decor. Custom configuration is available upon request, but may delay build time and could include extra costs. Visit the 'Configure' page to learn more about our currently supported Mini-ATX type cases or custom configuration.

Choose Your Tablet

Along with custom cases, BlackBox can also be configured with different tablet devices. Most tablets are supported and requests can be made for a specific tablet which may not be listed. Unfortunately, configuration cannot be done on existing devices, but simple to follow directions will be provided on how this may be accomplished. Similar to the cases, currently supported tablets can be found on the 'Configure' page.
